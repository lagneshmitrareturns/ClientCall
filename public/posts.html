Why Marriage Gets Delayed Even When D1 Looks Perfect

Category: Marriage Views: 128 Engagement: High

Many people obsess over the D1 chart and still feel confused when marriage does not materialise. The truth is, D1 alone never tells the complete story. What actually blocks marriage often hides deeper...

Marriage is not denied simply because the 7th house looks weak. In real cases, delay is usually caused by a mismatch between D9 promise, running dasha, and the psychological readiness of the native.

I have repeatedly observed charts where Venus is strong in D1, yet marriage keeps slipping away. When checked deeper, the D9 either shows detachment, karmic debt, or an unfinished emotional loop from past lives.

Another overlooked factor is the Moon. If the Moon is under affliction or placed in dusthana houses, the native may unconsciously reject commitment even while desiring it.

This is why blanket remedies fail. Without decoding D9, dasha, and emotional signature together, marriage timing remains misunderstood.
